魏欣然，主要研究兴趣为人工智能驱动的高性能科学计算和科学模型。她于2021年毕业并加入微软亚洲研究院，曾作为核心成员参与研发微软第一个云科学计算服务，利用GPU分布式集群大幅提升密度泛函理论的计算速度和体系规模。此外，她还曾参与多智能体强化学习的资源优化平台（MARO）的搭建和算法优化工作。已在包括ICML、ICLR、NeurIPS等在内国际会议和期刊上发表10余篇论文。


Wei Xinran's research interests lie in 'AI-driven high-performance scientific computing and scientific models'. She graduated in 2021 and joined Microsoft Research Asia. As a core member, she participated in the development of Microsoft's first cloud scientific computing service, leveraging distributed GPU clusters to significantly improve the computational speed and scale of density functional theory. She also participated in the development and algorithm optimization of the Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning Resource Optimization Platform (MARO). She has published over 10 papers in international conferences and journals, including ICML, ICLR, and NeurIPS.
